# Utah Unemployment Insurance — DWS

## Agency
Utah Department of Workforce Services (DWS)
- Website: jobs.utah.gov
- Online portal: jobs.utah.gov (file and certify online 24/7)
- Phone: 801-526-4400 (Salt Lake area) / 1-888-848-0688 (statewide; Mon–Fri 8am–5pm MT)

## Basic Eligibility
- Separated from employment through no fault of your own
- Worked in Utah during the base period (first four of last five completed quarters)
- Earned at least $3,000 in the base period across at least two quarters
- Able to work, available for work, and actively seeking employment

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: ~$725/week (among the higher caps in the Mountain West; adjusted annually)
- **Benefit rate**: Approximately 60% of your average weekly wage, up to cap
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks

## How to Apply
1. Apply online at jobs.utah.gov (24/7; preferred)
2. Apply by phone: 801-526-4400 or 1-888-848-0688
3. File within the first week after job loss
4. One-week waiting period before first payment
5. Certify every two weeks online or by phone

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Triggered when Utah receives a presidential major disaster declaration
- Covers self-employed workers and those ineligible for regular UI
- Apply through DWS within 30 days of DUA announcement date
- DWS posts disaster-specific updates at jobs.utah.gov

## Work Search Requirements
- Must make at least 4 verifiable job contacts per week (Utah has a higher requirement than most states)
- Log contacts in the online system
- Work search may be waived for counties under a federal disaster declaration

## Key Resources
- DWS disaster page: jobs.utah.gov (search "disaster")
- Utah American Job Centers: Free job placement, career counseling, retraining; jobs.utah.gov/locations
- SBA Disaster Loans: sba.gov/disaster
- FEMA Individual Assistance: disasterassistance.gov (apply within 60 days)
