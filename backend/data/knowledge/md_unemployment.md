# Maryland Unemployment Insurance — DLLR

## Agency
Maryland Department of Labor, Licensing and Regulation (DLLR)
- Website: dllr.state.md.us/employment/unemployment.shtml
- Online portal: mduiportal.labor.maryland.gov (BEACON — file and certify online)
- Phone: 410-949-0022 or 1-800-827-4839 (Mon–Fri 8am–4pm ET)

## Basic Eligibility
- Separated from employment through no fault of your own
- Worked in Maryland during the base period
- Earned at least $900 in the base period across at least two quarters
- Able to work, available, and actively seeking work

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: $430/week
- **Benefit rate**: ~1/24th of wages in your two highest-earning base period quarters
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks

## How to Apply
1. File online at mduiportal.labor.maryland.gov (BEACON system; 24/7)
2. File by phone: 410-949-0022
3. File within the first week after job loss
4. One-week unpaid waiting period before first payment
5. Certify every two weeks through BEACON

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Triggered when Maryland receives a presidential major disaster declaration
- Covers self-employed workers and those ineligible for regular UI (agricultural workers, farm operators)
- Apply through Maryland DLLR within 30 days of DUA announcement
- DLLR posts disaster updates at labor.maryland.gov

## Work Search Requirements
- Must make at least 1 job contact per week
- Log job search activities in BEACON
- Work search requirements may be waived during disaster recovery for affected counties
