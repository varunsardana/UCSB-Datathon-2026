# Minnesota Unemployment Insurance — DEED

## Agency
Minnesota Department of Employment and Economic Development (DEED)
- Website: uimn.org
- Online portal: uimn.org (file and certify online 24/7)
- Phone: 651-296-3644 (Twin Cities area) / 1-877-898-9090 (Greater MN; Mon–Fri 8am–4:30pm CT)

## Basic Eligibility
- Separated from employment through no fault of your own
- Worked in Minnesota during the base period
- Earned at least $1,000 in at least one base period quarter
- Able to work, available for work, and actively seeking employment

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: $857/week (one of the highest in the Midwest)
- **Benefit rate**: Approximately 50% of your average weekly wage, up to the cap
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks

## How to Apply
1. Apply online at uimn.org (24/7; fastest method)
2. Apply by phone: 651-296-3644 (Twin Cities) or 1-877-898-9090 (Greater MN)
3. Apply within the first week after job loss
4. No waiting week in Minnesota — first payable week is week one
5. Certify every week online or by phone

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Activated when Minnesota receives a presidential major disaster declaration
- Covers workers and self-employed individuals not eligible for regular UI
- Apply through DEED within 30 days of DUA announcement
- DEED posts disaster-specific notices at uimn.org and deed.mn.gov

## Minnesota-Specific
- **No waiting week**: Unlike most states, Minnesota pays from the first week — no unpaid waiting period
- **Dependency benefits**: Additional $12/week per dependent (up to $60/week extra) — one of the few states with this

## Work Search Requirements
- Must make 3 job contacts per week (can vary; check uimn.org for current requirements)
- Log contacts in the online system
- Work search may be waived during disaster recovery for affected areas

## Key Resources
- DEED disaster page: deed.mn.gov
- Minnesota WorkForce Centers: Free career services, retraining, job placement; mn.gov/deed/job-seekers/job-help
- SBA Disaster Loans: sba.gov/disaster
- FEMA Individual Assistance: disasterassistance.gov (apply within 60 days)
