# Connecticut Unemployment Insurance — DOL

## Agency
Connecticut Department of Labor (CT DOL)
- Website: ct.gov/dol
- ReEmployCT portal: reemployct.dol.state.ct.us (file and certify online)
- Phone: 860-263-6000 (Mon–Fri 9am–4pm ET)

## Basic Eligibility
- Lost job through no fault of your own
- Worked in Connecticut during the base period (first four of last five completed quarters)
- Earned at least $600 in the base period (must have worked in at least two quarters)
- Able, available, and actively seeking work

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: $742/week
- **Benefit rate**: ~60% of your average weekly wage, up to cap
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks
- Additional dependency allowances available if you have dependents

## How to Apply
1. File online at ReEmployCT portal (reemployct.dol.state.ct.us) — available 24/7
2. Phone filing available at 860-263-6000 (limited hours)
3. File during the first week after job loss
4. One-week waiting period applies before first payment
5. Certify weekly online through ReEmployCT

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Triggered when Connecticut receives a federal major disaster declaration
- Covers self-employed workers and those who exhausted regular UI
- Apply through CT DOL within 30 days of DUA announcement
- Check ct.gov/dol for disaster-specific announcements

## Work Search Requirements
- Must make 3 verified job contacts per week
- Log all contacts in the ReEmployCT system
- Work search may be waived during disaster recovery periods for affected areas — check announcements
