# Arizona Unemployment Insurance — DES

## Agency
Arizona Department of Economic Security (DES)
- Website: des.az.gov/unemployment
- Phone: 1-877-600-2722 (Mon–Fri 8am–5pm MST)
- Online portal: PUA.az.gov / des.az.gov (file and certify online 24/7)

## Basic Eligibility
- Lost job through no fault of your own (layoff, disaster-related closure, business shutdown)
- Worked in Arizona during the base period (first four of last five completed calendar quarters)
- Earned sufficient wages during the base period (DES calculates based on your earnings history)
- Physically able to work and actively seeking employment

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: $320/week
- **Minimum weekly benefit**: $187/week
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks (may vary with state unemployment rate)
- Note: Arizona has one of the lower benefit caps in the US — budget accordingly

## How to Apply
1. File online at des.az.gov (fastest; available 24/7)
2. File by phone: 1-877-600-2722
3. File within the first week after job loss — there is a one-week waiting period before first payment
4. Certify (confirm eligibility) every week online or by phone

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Triggered when Arizona receives a federal major disaster declaration
- Available to workers and self-employed individuals not covered by regular UI
- Apply through DES within 30 days of the DUA announcement
- DES typically opens a special hotline after a disaster declaration — check des.az.gov for updates

## Work Search Requirements
- Must actively search for work each week (typically 1+ job contacts per week)
- Record your job search activities — DES may audit them
- During federally declared disasters, work search may be temporarily waived for affected areas

## Key Disaster-Specific Resources
- DES Disaster Recovery: des.az.gov (search "disaster")
- SBA Disaster Loans (for businesses): sba.gov/disaster
- FEMA Individual Assistance: disasterassistance.gov
- Arizona Workforce Connection Centers: Free job search, resume, and retraining help; azwfc.com
