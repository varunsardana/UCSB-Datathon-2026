# Missouri Unemployment Insurance — DOL

## Agency
Missouri Department of Labor and Industrial Relations
- Website: labor.mo.gov
- Online portal: UInteract at uinteract.labor.mo.gov (file and certify online)
- Phone: 1-800-320-2519 (Mon–Fri 8am–5pm CT)

## Basic Eligibility
- Separated from employment through no fault of your own
- Worked in Missouri during the base period
- Earned at least $1,500 in at least one quarter of the base period
- Able to work, available for work, and actively seeking employment

## Benefit Amounts
- **Maximum weekly benefit**: $320/week (one of the lower caps nationally)
- **Duration**: Up to 20 weeks (Missouri has a shorter maximum than most states)
- Note: 20-week maximum is below the national norm — plan retraining early if displaced

## How to Apply
1. File online at uinteract.labor.mo.gov (24/7; preferred)
2. File by phone: 1-800-320-2519
3. File within the first week after job loss
4. One-week waiting period before first payment
5. Certify every two weeks through UInteract

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Activated when Missouri receives a presidential major disaster declaration (Missouri has significant flood and severe storm disaster history)
- Covers self-employed workers and those ineligible for regular UI
- Apply through Missouri DOL within 30 days of DUA announcement
- DOL posts disaster updates at labor.mo.gov

## Work Search Requirements
- Must make at least 3 job contacts per week
- Record contacts in UInteract
- During declared disasters, Missouri may waive work search for affected counties

## Key Resources
- Missouri DOL disaster page: labor.mo.gov (search "disaster")
- Missouri Job Centers: Free career services, retraining; jobs.mo.gov
- SBA Disaster Loans: sba.gov/disaster
- FEMA Individual Assistance: disasterassistance.gov (apply within 60 days)
