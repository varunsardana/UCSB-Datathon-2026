# California Unemployment Insurance — EDD

## Basic Eligibility
To qualify for California Unemployment Insurance (UI):
- You must have lost your job through no fault of your own (layoff, business closure, disaster)
- You must have earned at least $1,300 in your highest-earning quarter of the base period
- You must be physically able to work and actively looking for work

## Benefit Amounts
- **Weekly benefit**: 60-70% of your earnings up to the weekly maximum
- **Maximum weekly benefit (2025)**: $450 per week
- **Duration**: Up to 26 weeks in most cases
- **Base period**: Your earnings in the first four of the last five completed calendar quarters

## How to File a Claim
1. File online at UI.EDD.ca.gov (fastest, 24/7)
2. File by phone: 1-800-300-5616 (Mon-Fri 8am-5pm)
3. You must file within the first week after you stop working
4. **One-week waiting period** before your first payment (no payment for week 1)
5. You must certify (confirm eligibility) every two weeks online or by phone

## Disaster Unemployment Assistance (DUA) — California
If you don't qualify for regular UI (self-employed, insufficient earnings):
- DUA is triggered when California receives a federal disaster declaration
- Provides weekly benefits equivalent to state UI minimums
- Apply through EDD within 30 days of DUA announcement
- Call 1-800-300-5616 and mention you are filing due to a disaster

## Paid Leave Options
- **California Paid Family Leave (PFL)**: 60-70% of wages for up to 8 weeks if caring for ill family members affected by disaster
- **State Disability Insurance (SDI)**: If you are personally injured in a disaster and cannot work

## Work Search Requirements
While collecting UI, you must:
- Actively look for work each week
- Accept suitable work offers
- Report any earnings during your claim period
- During declared disasters, California may temporarily waive work search requirements for affected areas

## Key EDD Resources
- Website: edd.ca.gov
- Unemployment hotline: 1-800-300-5616
- EDD office locations: find at edd.ca.gov/Office_Locator
- SDI/PFL: 1-800-480-3287
- America's Job Center of California (AJCC): Free job placement and retraining services; find locations at ajcc.ca.gov
